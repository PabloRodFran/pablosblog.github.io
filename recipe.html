Burritos with chili

Recipe

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 4 burritos

Ingredients

ingredients quantity
wheat tortillas 4
minced meat 250gr
onion 1/2
green pepper 1/2
red pepper 1/2
tablespoons sweet corn. 4
tablespoons of tomato sauce 4
cooked beans 100gr
grated cheese 150gr
lettuce leaves. few
pickled gherkins 2
tabasco few drops
oil, salt and pepper few

Recipe

Step 1: Wash the red pepper , remove the peduncle and the seeds and cut it into strips. Do the same with the green pepper.
Step 2: Peel the onion and cut it into strips too.
Step 3: In a pan with a little oil and salt check the onion , the red pepper and green pepper and pepper , set fire to high temperature and saute about 3 minutes. The idea is that the vegetables are crispy and with a light golden touch. Reservation.
Step 4: In that same pan add a little more oil , put the heat on medium heat and pour the minced meat along with a little salt , freshly ground black pepper and tabasco if you want a spicy touch. Skip it about 5 minutes or until it stops being rosita.
Step 5: Cover it with water and simmer for 15-20 minutes to make it very tender. .
Step 6: Add the beans , corn and tomato sauce , stir everything well and let it cook 5 minutes all together.
Step 7: Heat the tortillas a little , for this you can do it putting one by one on a plate and just 10-15 seconds in the microwave, in a pan over medium heat until browned a little on both sides, or in the oven about 1 minute per each side at 200 ° C and putting them on a rack with baking paper in the highest area.
Step 8: Ride the burritos, for this place the lettuce leaves and the pickles (cut them in slices previously), over the mixture of meat and beans that we have prepared, a little mixture of onion and peppers and finally the cheese . Close the burritos by placing one side over the other and also closing one end inwards.
Step 9: If you like to serve them like this directly they are delicious, but if you prefer to bake them a little or even already closed, pass them through a pan so that the cheese melts, I recommend you not to let them let the lettuce because it releases a lot of liquid hot.
