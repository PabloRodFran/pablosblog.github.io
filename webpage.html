Italy

Italy, officially the Italian Republic is a European country consisting of a peninsula delimited by the Alps and surrounded by several islands. Its territory, with capital in Rome , is divided into twenty regions formed by 110 provinces .
Most of Italy corresponds to the biome of Mediterranean forest , although are also present temperate hardwood forest , between the Po Valley and the Apennines, and temperate coniferous forest in the Alps.
